# Quantus V2+ — Vertical Modules

## Overview

Quantus V2+ operates across 9 specialised verticals, each with dedicated AI logic, vendor networks, and compliance requirements.

---

## 1. Aviation
- **Use Cases:** Private jet acquisition, charter management, aircraft maintenance
- **Commission:** 2.5%
- **Key Features:** Aircraft specifications matching, regulatory compliance, maintenance scheduling

## 2. Marine
- **Use Cases:** Yacht acquisition, marina management, crew staffing
- **Commission:** 5%
- **Key Features:** Vessel comparison, port logistics, flag state compliance

## 3. Legal
- **Use Cases:** Corporate structuring, estate planning, intellectual property
- **Commission:** 7.5%
- **Key Features:** Document drafting, jurisdictional analysis, contract review

## 4. Finance
- **Use Cases:** Wealth management, investment advisory, tax optimisation
- **Commission:** 5%
- **Key Features:** Portfolio tracking, risk assessment, regulatory reporting

## 5. Lifestyle
- **Use Cases:** Luxury travel, events, personal shopping, property management
- **Commission:** 10–15%
- **Key Features:** Preference learning, vendor curation, experience coordination

## 6. Medical
- **Use Cases:** Medical travel, specialist consultations, wellness retreats
- **Commission:** 5–8%
- **Key Features:** Provider matching, medical records coordination, travel logistics

## 7. Staffing
- **Use Cases:** Household staff, personal assistants, estate management
- **Commission:** 20%
- **Key Features:** Background verification, skill matching, contract management

## 8. Logistics
- **Use Cases:** Freight, customs, relocation, secure transport
- **Commission:** 3–5%
- **Key Features:** Route optimisation, customs documentation, real-time tracking

## 9. Partnerships
- **Use Cases:** Strategic alliances, joint ventures, vendor networks
- **Commission:** Variable
- **Key Features:** Partner matching, due diligence, agreement management

---

## Module Architecture

Each module follows a consistent pattern:
1. **AI Classification** — Incoming requests are categorised by vertical
2. **Sourcing Engine** — AI identifies matching vendors/options
3. **Scoring** — Options scored on cost, quality, availability, risk
4. **Outreach** — Automated vendor communication
5. **Negotiation** — AI-assisted negotiation support
6. **Completion** — Document generation, invoicing, commission logging
