# Quantus V2+ — Revenue Model & Pricing

| Tier | Monthly | Annual (20% off) | Target |

| Starter | $29/mo | $276/yr | Individual users |
| Professional | $149/mo | $1,428/yr | Power users, small offices |

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Subscriptions (Recurring)
Primary revenue via Stripe Hosted Checkout. Annual plans offer 20% discount.

### 2. Commission Revenue (Transactional)
Earned on completed deals, varying by vertical:

| Vertical | Commission Rate |
|----------|----------------|
| Staffing | 20% |
| Wellness / Lifestyle | 10–15% |
| Legal | 7.5% |
| Medical | 5–8% |
| Marine | 5% |
| Finance | 5% |
| Logistics | 3–5% |
| Real Estate | 3–5% |
| Aviation | 2.5% |

### 3. Priority Deal Processing
One-time $49 surcharge per deal for expedited AI orchestration (+20 priority score boost).

### 4. Platform Fees (Stripe Connect)
10% platform fee on vendor products sold through the marketplace.

### 5. Usage Overage
Pay-as-you-go charges when users exceed tier limits (deal counts, API calls, quantum shots).

## Upsell Automation

Dismissible banners trigger at usage thresholds:
- **75%** — Informational upgrade suggestion
- **90%** — Urgent warning
- **100%** — Critical limit reached, functionality restricted

## Growth Engine

- **Referral Program:** Unique `QUANTUSV2-XXXXXX` codes, credit rewards for referrer and referred
- **Lead Nurture Drip:** 3-email automated sequence (Day 1 welcome → Day 3 tips → Day 7 upgrade CTA)
- **Newsletter Capture:** Landing page lead collection
- **Social Proof:** Animated stat counters + trust badges (ISO 27001, SOC 2)
