## Tone of Voice

- **Authority:** Speak with quiet confidence, not loudness
- **Discretion:** Never oversell; let capability speak
- **Precision:** Every word chosen deliberately
- **Exclusivity:** "Select," "curated," "bespoke" — not "cheap" or "deal"

## Logo & Visual Elements

- Cinematic page transitions (Framer Motion)
- Three.js particle effects on hero sections
- Remotion-generated video backgrounds
- Gold gradient dividers between sections
